Objectives of our business

  1. Serve a minimum of 500 clients within the first year.
  2. Establish partnerships with local health care providers, senior centers, and community organizations.
  3. Develop a sustainable funding model through grants, donations, and fundraising events
  4. Recruit and train minimum of 50 volunteers within the first year

Business Goals

  1. As the business we aim on increasing donations, by encouraging the website visitors to make online donations to support the meals on wheels program
  2. We aim on recruiting volunteers, attract and recruit volunteers to help with meal delivery, fundraising, and other actvities
  3. We are trying to increase mail requests, by providing an easy way for seniors and individuals with disablities to requests meal delivery services
  4. Establish meals on wheels as a leading provider of meal delivery services for seniors and individuals with disablities
  5. Educate website visitors about the meals on wheels program, services, and benefits.

    6. Project scope

    Target audience:Elderly, diabled people. People that can not prepare their own meals and people that are homeless.

    Meal delivery:Our aim is to provide healthy meals to the doorstep of the needy.

    Social interaction: We want to make our customers feel at home by providing compasion and social interation to eliminate the feelings of lonliness and isolation.

    Wellness centers:We care about our customers that much that we want to find out their mental health, check their sugar levels, check their diabetes as well.
